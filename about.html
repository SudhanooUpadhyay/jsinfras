ABOUT US



OVERVIEW

JS Infrastructure consultants Pvt. Ltd. subsidy of JS Environics Consultants Pvt. Ltd. (JS) is a multidisciplinary consulting firm dealing in Infrastructure, Environmental & Social Consulting projects. It was established in May 2012 to acquire Honourable, Respectable & Leadership position in the field of Infrastructure, Environmental & Social Consulting Services within India and Neighbouring countries.

WHAT WE DO

JS Infrastructure consultants Pvt. Ltd. provides integrated solutions to complex problems in Infrastructure. The core focal issues being addressed are End to end consulting for design, operation and management of highway projects and rural roads. Design and Development consulting for rail road and tunnel projects is a core area we are focused in.

MISSION

JSECPL is completely independent with no affiliations to construction firms , suppliers or financial institutions. Objectivity through independence is a key aspect of this philosophy- JSECPL exclusively serves its clients interests. JSECPL strives for “market leadership through quality” in all its business areas. Our highly experienced specialists are supported by efficient organization structures, comprehensive management procedures, state-of-the-art technology and fully integrated knowledge management systems. Tailor made solutions addressing client’s specific needs and reliability on quality, cost and schedule are essential for JSECPL. Priority is given to innovation , cost effectiveness, sustainability and environmental compatibility.
